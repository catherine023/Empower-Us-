Our History

Discover the moments that shaped Empower Us+ and helped us grow into a movement grounded in community, care, and creativity.

for youth-led change and representation.

By 2022, we expanded our outreach with workshops, school events, and collaborations with nonprofits.

Today, we continue to build tools and spaces that help elevate marginalized voices through digital design and action.
